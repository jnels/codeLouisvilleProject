About Joel

Bio:

Joel W. Nelson spent much of his childhood in the sub-Saharan countries of Burkina Faso and Cote d'Ivoire. He has an MFA in Poetry from Spalding University and lives with his wife and son in Louisville, KY. His poems may be found in the Bellevue Literary Review, Found Poetry Review, The Louisville Review, and A Narrow Fellow.

In 2015, he successfully completed the Front-End track in Code Louisville, an initiative to train Louisville-area residents for web development jobs.
