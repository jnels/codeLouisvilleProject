About Joel

Bio

Joel W. Nelson spent much of his childhood in the sub-Saharan countries of Burkina Faso and Cote d'Ivoire. He has an MFA in Poetry from Spalding University and lives with his wife and son in Louisville, KY. His poems may be found in the Bellevue Literary Review, Found Poetry Review, The Louisville Review, and A Narrow Fellow.

In 2015, he participated in the Front-End Development track for Code Louisville, an initiative to train Louisville-area residents for web development jobs. In the future, he hopes to land a career in web development while continuing to write and publish his poetry. His ultimate goal is to find creative ways to blend the two fields.
